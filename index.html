How Your Donation Saves Lives

Every naira goes directly to verified medical treatment — no middlemen, no hidden fees.

01

Browse Verified Cases

Explore medical cases submitted by certified Nigerian pediatricians. Each case includes full medical details, treatment plans, and transparent funding goals.

02

Give Securely Online

Donate via card, bank transfer, or digital wallet. One-time or recurring — every amount makes a real difference. Instant tax receipt emailed to you.

03

Track the Healing Journey

Follow real-time updates as the child receives treatment. Photos, hospital reports, and a celebration when they recover — fully funded by donors like you.
